Angular works

VeganEats

VeganEats is an app designed to search exclusively for vegan recipes via a search bar by entering the desired ingredient or dish. Initially, when you start the application, a search will be made for 12 recipes from which you can take inspiration. Once the search has been carried out, the recipes will be displayed and by clicking on them, the details will then be shown such as the preparation time, the ingredients and other useful information for creating the dish.

Books Finder

This simple application allows the user by entering a literary genre that best suits his tastes in the appropriate search bar, to find a series of books relating to the genre chosen by the same. Furthermore, once the required titles have been found, it is possible, by clicking on them, to know the description of the title to learn more about the content and the cover of the selected book.

jobs finder

Jobs finder is an app designed to search for jobs based on your position and professional level that can be selected using the appropriate checkboxes. Initially, when starting the application, a job search will be carried out with the default category: Engineering (with small modifications it will be possible to search also in other fields). Once the search has been carried out, the various jobs found will be displayed and by clicking on the "view Job" button it will be possible to view all the information concerning the job and, using another button or by clicking on the company name, view the information on the agency.
